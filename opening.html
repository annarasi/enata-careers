Automation and Robotics Engineer

Expert in robotics to fully automatize and connect our robots to the ERP production system.

Apply Now

Responsibilities

  • Automation of composite parts production line including trimming and pick-and-place of fabric plies and hydraulic press.

  • Setup hardware.

  • Program the robots.

  • Develop and add IOT (raspberry pi like) devices to control the robots and various devices in Rust.

  • Connect the IOT devices to the JAVA based online ERP system.

Requirements

  • Minimum of 6 years experience in automation and robot programming.

  • Good Kuka robot knowledge, including KRC2 and KRC3 controllers.

  • Good level of English.

  • Develop and add IOT (raspberry pi like) devices to control the robots and various devices in Rust.Knowledge of Rust programing.

  • Optional: Knowlegde of Java programing.
